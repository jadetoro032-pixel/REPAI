# RepAI Hackathon Demo Script

## 3-Minute Flow

1. Open the Demo Control Center and show the 4/4 readiness rail.
2. Show the Two RepAI tools panel: Meeting Delegate for calls and Staff Support for assigned work.
3. Show the Meeting capability matrix: Copilot agent works now, call joining needs a Teams bot/custom engine layer, decisions are risk-gated.
4. Jeremiah is double-booked for the Enterprise Launch Product Sync.
5. RepAI joins the demo as a disclosed delegate: "I am RepAI, attending as Jeremiah's delegate."
6. A participant asks by voice: "What signoff do we need before launch approval?"
7. RepAI listens through the voice pipeline, answers from approved knowledge, speaks using either the active role voice profile or a user-selected browser voice, and cites the Launch Readiness Policy.
8. A participant asks for a risky pricing approval.
9. RepAI refuses to approve and escalates to Jeremiah/Finance.
10. Switch to Staff Support and type `@RepAI summarize the onboarding document and create next steps`.
11. Mock Work IQ retrieves email, Teams, calendar, SharePoint, and policy context.
12. RepAI classifies the item, drafts or completes the action, and writes an audit log.
13. Open Judge Test Mode and explain how judges can test with prompts, tenant documents, or their own Work IQ/Fabric IQ/Foundry tools.

## Key Line

Copilot helps a person do work. RepAI can be assigned work as a governed digital staff member.
